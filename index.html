Frequently Asked Questions

What is the optimal age for livestock castration?

The ideal age varies by species: calves at 2-3 months, lambs at 1-2 weeks, and goats at 8-12 weeks. Early castration generally results in less stress and faster recovery.

What does the recovery process involve?

Most animals return to normal feeding within 24 hours. Complete healing occurs in 10-14 days. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and 24/7 support during the recovery period.

How do you ensure a stress-free procedure?

We use modern pain management protocols, experienced handlers, and efficient techniques to minimize stress. Our mobile service eliminates transportation anxiety.

What are your service area boundaries?

We serve all of Southeast Texas, including Beaumont, Port Arthur, Orange, and surrounding counties within a 100-mile radius. Travel fees may apply for locations beyond our primary service area.

What is your pricing structure?

Rates vary based on herd size, location, and specific requirements. We offer volume discounts and can provide detailed quotes after assessing your needs. All services include post-procedure support.
